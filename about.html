About

I work best with teams who care about testing, iteration, and scaling what works.

  • Collaborative: Working closely with marketing teams and stakeholders

Background

With a background in Finance, I bring a unique perspective to copywriting. I understand the numbers behind the words, which allows me to create copy that not only converts but also aligns with business objectives and ROI goals.

My experience includes supporting high-spend Meta Ads teams managing $200K+/day in ad spend, working with 7–9 figure eCommerce brands, and creating copy for paid ads, email sequences, and landing pages that drive measurable results.
